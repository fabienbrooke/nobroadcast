No Broadcast are a three-piece hailing from Christchurch, NZ. They create high dynamic rock music with a strong sense of immersion for the listener that is reminiscent of HDU, Jakob and Kerretta. After the release of their defining self-titled debut album, they are on their biggest tour yet.
